# Welcome to the Hasfy documentation

Hasfy is the French platform for **IT fleet management and monitoring**, designed for IT teams, managed service providers (MSPs), and businesses of all sizes. This documentation will guide you through getting started, configuring, and running Hasfy on a daily basis.

## Quick Start

- **[Introduction](/introduction)**: Discover what Hasfy is, its key features, and who it's for.
- **[Quick Start](/quickstart)**: Create your account, add your first piece of equipment, and install the agent in just a few minutes.
- **[Configuration](/configuration/onboarding)**: Set up your organization, invite your team, and customize your workspace.

## Core Features

- **[Equipment](/core-features/equipments)**: Manage your complete fleet inventory: adding, editing, tags, lifecycle, and real-time metrics.
- **[Support & Tickets](/core-features/assistance)**: Create and track support tickets with assignment, priorities, conversations, and a resolution workflow.
- **[Dashboard & KPIs](/core-features/kpis)**: Visualize your fleet's health, resolution rates, and key performance indicators.
- **[Calendar](/core-features/calendar)**: Plan maintenance, interventions, and sync your team's schedule.
- **[Notifications](/core-features/notifications)**: Get real-time alerts on the status of your equipment and the progress of your tickets.
- **[Users & Groups](/core-features/usermanagement)**: Invite members, assign roles, and manage access permissions.

## Advanced Features

- **[AI Assistant](/advanced-features/ia)**: Optional add-on powered by Mistral AI: ticket summaries, solution suggestions, and equipment diagnostics.

## Security & Integrations

- **[Integrations](/integrations)**: Connect Hasfy to Mollie and the tools in your ecosystem.
- **[API](/api)**: Programmatically access your data via Hasfy's secure REST API.

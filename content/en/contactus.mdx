# Contact Us

We're here to help! Whether you need assistance, want a demo, or have suggestions, several channels are available to you.

## Technical support

### From the application

The fastest way to get help is to contact support directly from within Hasfy:

1. Click the **"?"** icon at the bottom right of the interface
2. Select **"Contact support"**
3. Describe your issue in as much detail as possible
4. The support team automatically receives your account context (plan, organization, browser)

### By email

Send your request to **support@hasfy.fr**, including:

- Your Hasfy account **email address**
- Your **organization name**
- A **detailed description** of the issue or question
- **Screenshots**, if relevant
- The **browser** and **operating system** you're using (in case of a bug)

### Response times

All Hasfy Pro accounts get a **guaranteed response time of 24 business hours** by email and chat.

Urgent requests (platform inaccessible, data loss) are handled as a priority.

## Request a demo

Want to discover Hasfy before signing up? We offer personalized demonstrations:

1. Send an email to **sales@hasfy.fr** with:
   - Your **company** name
   - Your **role** (CIO, IT Manager, Technician, etc.)
   - The **size of your IT fleet**
   - Your **main needs** (inventory, monitoring, ticketing, etc.)
2. We'll get back to you within **24 hours** to schedule a time slot
3. The demo lasts about **30 minutes** and is held via video call
4. We'll set up a **pre-configured test environment** so you can try Hasfy hands-on

> **Alternative**: You can also start right away with the **14-day free trial** at [app.hasfy.fr](https://app.hasfy.fr) — €0 at signup, nothing charged before the trial ends.

## Suggestions and feedback

Your feedback is essential to improving Hasfy. Share your ideas:

- **Email**: feedback@hasfy.fr
- **Roadmap**: vote for your priority features on our [Notion roadmap](https://dynamic-wrench-36e.notion.site/ROADMAP-HASFY-2025-1854c935aab18101974dd846e8effeac)
- **From the application**: use the "💡 Suggestion" button in the help menu

Every suggestion is read and reviewed by the product team. The most requested features are prioritized in our roadmap.

## Reporting a security issue

If you've discovered a security vulnerability, please report it responsibly:

- **Email**: security@hasfy.fr
- Do not disclose the vulnerability publicly before we respond
- We commit to responding within **24 business hours**
- Verified reports are rewarded and credited

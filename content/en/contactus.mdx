# Contact Us

We're here to help! Several channels are available to reach us.

## Live chat

The fastest way to get help is our live chat, powered by Crisp. You can open it in two ways from the application:

- By clicking the chat button at the top right of the screen

![Hasfy chat button](/images/contact/contact-button.jpg)

- By clicking your **profile** in the sidebar, then **"Need help"**

![“Need help” button in the profile menu](/images/contact/help-button.jpg)

A conversation opens directly with our team, who usually replies within a few minutes during business hours.

## By email

Prefer writing? Send us a message at **contact@hasfy.fr**: general questions, support requests, suggestions, or partnerships. Our team makes sure your request reaches the right person.

## By video call

Need to talk to us directly, for example for a demo or personalized guidance? Book a slot that works for you at [cal.com/hasfy/30min](https://cal.com/hasfy/30min). You'll automatically receive a video call link by email.

# About Hasfy

## Our mission

Hasfy's mission is to **simplify IT fleet management** for IT teams of all sizes. We believe that every organization, whether a 10-person SMB or a 5,000-employee company, deserves a modern, accessible, and high-performing tool to manage its IT assets.

## Who we are

Hasfy is a **French SaaS** built by a team passionate about the intersection of technology and user experience. Born from the observation that existing IT fleet management tools (ITSM, RMM) are often either too complex, too limited, or excessively expensive, Hasfy takes a different approach:

- **Easy to get started with**: guided onboarding and an intuitive interface let you become productive in just a few minutes
- **Comprehensive without being overwhelming**: the essential features (inventory, monitoring, ticketing, calendar, KPIs) are brought together in a single, coherent tool
- **Simple pricing**: a single plan billed per seat (€150/month at the Early Access rate), with no hidden fees or long-term commitment
- **Data hosted in France**: OVH infrastructure, native GDPR compliance, guaranteed data sovereignty

## Our values

### Simplicity

We design every feature by asking ourselves: "Could a busy technician use this without reading the manual?" Technical complexity is absorbed by the platform, not by the user.

### Transparency

Our pricing is published, our roadmap is shared, and how we handle data is documented. No surprises, no hidden terms.

### Reliability

An IT fleet management tool needs to work when you need it — especially in a crisis. We constantly invest in the stability, performance, and redundancy of our infrastructure.

### Closeness

Hasfy is a French company that serves its users. Our support is responsive, human, and available in French. We listen to feedback from our customers to evolve the product.

## Our technology

Hasfy is built on a modern, proven technology stack:

- **Web application**: Next.js for a fast, responsive experience
- **Database**: PostgreSQL via Supabase for reliability and performance
- **Monitoring**: Elasticsearch and Elastic Agent for collecting and analyzing metrics
- **Desktop agent**: built with Tauri for a lightweight, cross-platform installer
- **Hosting**: OVH, French data centers

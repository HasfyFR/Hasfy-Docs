# Technician Marketplace

Hasfy's **Technician Marketplace** connects organizations that need technical interventions with a network of **qualified, vetted technicians**. Whether you're short on internal resources or need specific expertise, the marketplace lets you find the right technician quickly.

> **Availability**: The Technician Marketplace is included in the Hasfy Pro plan — no extra option to enable.

## When to use the marketplace

The marketplace is particularly useful in the following situations:

- **Temporary overload**: your internal team is overwhelmed and you need extra support for a short period
- **Specific expertise**: you need a specialist in an area your team doesn't cover (network, security, virtualization, etc.)
- **Geographic coverage**: you have equipment in an area far from your team
- **Urgent intervention**: a critical incident requires immediate attention and your team isn't available
- **No internal IT team**: you're a small business without a dedicated IT department

## Finding a technician

### Accessing the marketplace

1. From the main menu, go to **Marketplace &gt; Technicians**
2. The page displays the list of technicians available in your geographic area

### Search filters

Refine your search using the following filters:

| Filter | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Location** | City, region, or radius around an address |
| **Specialties** | Network, Systems, Security, Hardware, Software, Cloud, Printing, Telephony |
| **Availability** | Available now, this week, specific date |
| **Intervention type** | On-site, Remote |
| **Minimum rating** | Filter by average rating (e.g. 4 stars and above) |
| **Certifications** | Microsoft, Cisco, CompTIA, ITIL, etc. |
| **Rate** | Hourly rate range |

### Technician profile

Each technician has a detailed profile showing:

- **General information**: name, photo, location, service area
- **Specialties**: areas of expertise with level (beginner, experienced, expert)
- **Certifications**: verified professional certifications
- **Experience**: years of experience and number of interventions completed via Hasfy
- **Availability**: open slots for the coming weeks
- **Rates**: indicative hourly rate (the final rate is set in the quote)
- **Average rating**: 5-star rating based on customer reviews
- **Customer reviews**: detailed feedback from previous interventions

## Requesting a quote

### Request process

1. From a technician's profile (or from the list), click **"Request a quote"**
2. Fill out the request form:
   - **Intervention title**: a short description of the need
   - **Detailed description**: explain the problem or work to be done in as much detail as possible
   - **Intervention type**: on-site or remote
   - **Intervention address** *(if on-site)*: the exact location of the intervention
   - **Urgency**: Normal (within 7 days), Priority (within 48 hours), Urgent (within 24 hours)
   - **Affected equipment**: select the related equipment from your inventory
   - **Preferred date** *(optional)*: preferred date and time slot
   - **Attachments**: photos, screenshots, technical documentation
3. Click **"Send request"**

### Technician's response

The technician receives your request and has **48 hours** to respond with:

- **Acceptance with quote**: estimated amount, expected duration, conditions
- **Request for clarification**: additional questions before being able to provide a price
- **Decline**: the technician is unavailable or doesn't cover this type of request

### Approving the quote

Once you receive the quote, you can:

- **Accept**: the intervention is scheduled and appears in your calendar
- **Negotiate**: send a counter-proposal or changes
- **Decline**: the quote is archived and you can contact another technician

## Scheduling the intervention

Once the quote is accepted:

1. An **event** is automatically created in the Hasfy calendar
2. A **ticket** can be created or linked if not already done
3. The technician receives a **confirmation** with the intervention details
4. **Reminders** are sent before the intervention (24 hours and 1 hour before)

### Tracking the intervention

During and after the intervention, tracking is done from the associated ticket:

- The technician can add **notes** and **photos** of the intervention
- **Time spent** is logged for billing purposes
- The ticket status changes: In progress → Resolved
- An **intervention report** is generated automatically

## Ratings and reviews

### Rating a technician

After each completed intervention, you're invited to rate the technician:

1. **Overall rating**: from 1 to 5 stars
2. **Detailed criteria**:
   - **Punctuality**: adherence to agreed schedules
   - **Technical skill**: quality of the intervention
   - **Communication**: clarity of exchanges and reporting
   - **Value for money**: how well the rate matched the service
3. **Comment**: free text describing your experience
4. **Recommendation**: would you recommend this technician? (Yes/No)

> **Note**: Reviews are published after moderation and remain anonymous (only the organization's name is shown, not the reviewer's).

### Browsing reviews

On each technician's profile, reviews are displayed in reverse chronological order. You can filter by rating and intervention type.

## Becoming a technician on the marketplace

If you're an independent technician or IT service provider, you can join the Hasfy marketplace:

1. Create a Hasfy account with the **Technician** role
2. Go to **Marketplace &gt; My technician profile**
3. Complete your profile: specialties, certifications, service area, rates
4. Submit your application for verification
5. After approval by the Hasfy team (within 48-72 hours), your profile becomes visible on the marketplace

### Requirements

- Proof of identity
- Proof of professional activity (business registration number or equivalent)
- At least one recognized IT certification *(recommended but not required)*
- Professional liability insurance

## Billing

Billing for marketplace interventions is handled via **Mollie**, Hasfy's payment provider:

- Payment is secured and held in escrow until the intervention is validated
- The invoice is issued automatically after validation
- Invoices are accessible from **Settings &gt; Billing &gt; History**
- Technicians receive their payment within 7 business days after validation

For more details on billing, see the [Billing & Payment](/integrations/stripe) page.

# AI Assistant

Hasfy's **AI Assistant** is an optional add-on designed to save you time on two recurring tasks: quickly understanding a ticket and diagnosing a problem on a piece of equipment. Instead of re-reading a long thread of exchanges or starting an investigation from scratch, you can rely on AI to get a summary, resolution leads, or a diagnostic report in seconds.

## Ticket summaries

From any ticket, click **"AI Summary"** to generate a clear, concise summary of the ticket: all of its exchanges (both team-side and client-portal side) along with its current status.

- The summary is generated **on demand**: it isn't created automatically when the ticket is opened
- You can **regenerate** the summary at any time using the dedicated button, for example after new exchanges are added. A confirmation is requested before regenerating ("Regenerate AI summary? A new summary will consume AI tokens. Continue?")
- Each generation (or regeneration) **consumes AI tokens**, deducted from your monthly quota

This is especially useful for picking up an ongoing ticket, getting a quick recap before a meeting, or handing off context to a colleague.

## Solution suggestions

While working on a ticket, the AI Assistant can search your **organization's knowledge base** (which combines the documents you've added manually and your organization's already-resolved tickets, automatically re-indexed) to suggest relevant resolution leads.

- The assistant relies on semantic search (RAG) over your organization's indexed content, not on generic knowledge disconnected from your context
- Suggestions are therefore based on **what has already worked for you**, or on documentation you've added to Hasfy yourself
- The technician remains the sole decision-maker: the AI proposes leads, it doesn't resolve the ticket for you

## Equipment diagnostics

On a monitored equipment's record, you can click **"Generate an AI diagnostic report to analyze this equipment"**.

- The report is generated from the **data collected by the agent** on that piece of equipment (metrics, status, history)
- It helps you analyze an ongoing issue or better understand a machine's behavior, without manually combing through metric charts
- As with ticket summaries, generating a diagnostic is an **on-demand** action that consumes AI tokens

## Activation and pricing

The AI Assistant is an **optional add-on**, enabled or disabled via a **single, organization-wide toggle** in **Settings > Billing** (reserved to the **Owner** role). Once enabled, every member of the organization gets access to the AI features (summaries, suggestions, diagnostics), regardless of their role. There's no per-account activation.

## Model and data

### Model used

The AI Assistant's features are powered by **Mistral AI** (`mistral-small-latest`). No OpenAI GPT or Google Gemini models are involved in any processing.

### Data sent to Mistral

On each AI call, the following information is transmitted to the **Mistral API** (Mistral servers, outside the European Union):

- Ticket ID, title, status, priority, and description
- All ticket messages (content and timestamps)
- First name, last name, and email of the ticket creator
- First name, last name, and email of assigned technicians
- Attachment names *(not their content)*
- Name and type of the equipment linked to the ticket
- Ticket tags
- Relevant knowledge base documents (up to 800 characters per document)
- Similar already-resolved tickets (RAG context)

### Isolation between organizations

The context queries sent to Mistral are filtered by `organization_id` at the database level. One organization cannot access another organization's data through the AI.

### Result caching

AI-generated results are **stored in the database** within the ticket (`solutions_ai` column, JSON format). A second call on the same ticket returns the cached result without calling Mistral again, unless you click **"Regenerate"** (`forceRefresh=true`).

### Data processing agreement (DPA) with Mistral

> ⚠️ **No DPA (Data Processing Agreement) is currently signed with Mistral AI.** The Mistral API is used under **standard terms and conditions**, under which data transmitted via the API may be used by Mistral for service improvement. There is no contractual guarantee of non-training on your data.

**Recommendation**: if your organization handles sensitive data (client personal data, medical information, confidential data), we advise you to **not enable the AI add-on** until a DPA with Mistral is in place. Contact us via [Contact](/contactus) for an update on this matter.

# Billing & Payment (Mollie)

Hasfy relies on **Mollie** to handle payment and billing for your subscription. This page covers how to subscribe, pay, manage your seats, and view your invoices.

## Overview

Hasfy offers only **a single plan, Hasfy Pro**, billed **per seat** (administrator or technician account). There's no plan to choose and no upgrade/downgrade path: you simply adjust the **number of seats** based on the size of your IT team.

All payments and charges are processed by **Mollie**, which supports credit cards as well as other payment methods depending on your country.

## Subscribing

1. Create your account at **[app.hasfy.fr](https://app.hasfy.fr)**: signing up automatically starts your **14-day free trial**
2. Enter your payment details (credit card) to activate the direct debit mandate. **Nothing is charged before the trial ends**
3. Set the **number of seats** you need (one seat per administrator or technician account); each seat automatically unlocks 250 free standard user accounts
4. At the end of the trial, your subscription automatically switches to the current rate (currently €150/month per seat, Early Access rate)

See the [Pricing](/pricing) page for the full details on prices and conditions.

## Billing cycle

The Hasfy Pro subscription is billed **monthly**, on the anniversary of your subscription date. The charge via Mollie is collected automatically on each due date, using the payment method on file for your account.

## Free trial

- **14 days free**, **€0 at signup**
- A **credit card is required** to start the trial (direct debit mandate), but nothing is charged before the trial period ends
- **Cancellable at any time during the trial**, at no cost
- At the end of the trial, the subscription automatically starts at the current rate, with no action required on your part

See the "14-day free trial" section on the [Pricing](/pricing) page for the full details.

## Payment methods

Payments are handled by **Mollie**, which supports:

- **Credit cards** (Visa, Mastercard, etc.)
- **Other payment methods** depending on your billing country

The payment method is registered once when subscribing (or activating the free trial) and reused automatically for each due date. You can update it at any time from **Settings &gt; Billing &gt; Payment method**.

## Invoices

All your invoices are accessible from **Settings &gt; Billing &gt; Invoice history**:

- View the full history online
- Download as **PDF**
- Detail of each charge (number of seats, AI Assistant add-on if applicable, amount collected)

## Seat management

The number of seats can be adjusted at any time from **Settings &gt; Billing &gt; Seats**:

- **Adding a seat**: takes effect immediately and is billed **pro rata** for the remaining time in your current billing cycle
- **Removing a seat**: takes effect **at the next renewal**, and you keep using the seat until the end of the period you've already paid for, with no partial refund mid-cycle

Each seat added or removed automatically updates your included standard-user quota (250 accounts per seat).

## Client portal

Hasfy provides a **client portal** that you can give your own clients access to (especially useful for MSPs managing multiple organizations):

- Optional custom subdomain
- Read access to information relevant to your clients (equipment, tickets, etc.)

> **Billing your end clients**: to charge your clients online (card payment links, payment tracking), connect your own Mollie account via [Mollie Connect](/integrations/mollie-connect). Without this connection, billing your clients through the portal works in **manual mode**: you generate and send your invoices as PDFs, and payment is made by **bank transfer**.

## Cancelling your subscription

You can cancel your subscription at any time from **Settings &gt; Billing &gt; Cancel subscription**:

- Access to features is maintained until the **end of the current billing period**
- No further charges are collected after cancellation
- Make sure to export any important data before the end of that period (see [Data & Backups](/settings-security/databackup))

For any questions about data retention after cancellation, contact us via [Contact](/contactus).

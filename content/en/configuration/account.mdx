# Account management

The **Account** section lets you manage all your personal information and security preferences. Access it from the user menu at the top right of the interface, then **"My profile"**, or directly via **Settings &gt; Account**.

## Profile information

### Edit your information

From the **Profile** tab, you can edit:

- **First and last name**: displayed in the interface, ticket assignments, and notifications. Enter your full name as you would like it to appear to your colleagues
- **Job title / Role**: your job title (e.g. "IT Manager", "L2 Support Technician"). Displayed on your public profile within the organization
- **Phone number**: used for critical SMS notifications, included with the Hasfy Pro subscription
- **Bio** *(optional)*: a short description visible to other members of your organization

Click **"Save changes"** to confirm your updates.

### Profile picture (Avatar)

To add or change your avatar:

1. Click on your current profile picture (or the default icon)
2. Select an image file from your computer
3. Accepted formats: **JPG, PNG, WebP**
4. Maximum size: **2 MB**
5. The image is automatically cropped to a square format
6. Click **"Save"**

To remove your avatar and revert to the default icon, click **"Remove photo"**.

## Changing your email address

Changing the email address associated with your account requires a two-step verification:

1. Go to **Settings &gt; Account**
2. Enter your **new email address**
3. Confirm with your **current password**
4. Click **"Update email"**
5. A confirmation email is sent to the **new** address
6. Click the confirmation link in that email
7. Your email address is updated

> **Important**: Until you confirm the new address, your old email address remains active. If you don't receive the confirmation email, check your spam folder or click **"Resend link"**.

## Changing your password

To change your password:

1. Go to **Settings &gt; Account**
2. Enter your **current password**
3. Enter your **new password**: at least 8 characters (128 maximum). No complexity rule is enforced, but we recommend a long, unique password, for example one generated by a password manager
4. Confirm the new password
5. Click **"Update password"**

### Forgotten password

If you've forgotten your password:

1. On the login page, click **"Forgot password?"**
2. Enter the email address for your account
3. An email containing a reset link is sent
4. Click the link and set a new password. This link has a limited validity period

## Account notification preferences

Manage notifications specific to your account:

| Notification type | Description | Email | In-app |
|---------------------|-------------|-------|--------|
| Login from a new device | Alert if your account is used from an unknown device | ✅ | ✅ |
| Password change | Confirmation after a password change | ✅ | — |
| Email change | Confirmation of an address change | ✅ | — |
| Session expiry | Reminder when your session is about to expire | — | ✅ |

To change these preferences, go to **Settings &gt; Notifications**.

## Session security

Hasfy automatically applies the following security rules to your session, with no configuration needed:

- **Inactivity timeout**: your session expires after 24 hours without activity
- **Maximum lifetime**: a session expires after 7 days at the latest, even if you remain active in the meantime
- **Automatic sign-out of other sessions**: on every successful login, Hasfy automatically signs you out of all your other active sessions on your other devices

> **Security tip**: If you think your account has been compromised, change your password immediately. Your next login will automatically sign out any session opened by someone else.

## Account deletion

### Standard deletion (soft-delete)

You can delete your account from **Settings &gt; Account**, in the **"Danger zone"** section. Deletion can also be triggered by an administrator of your organization.

The deletion process runs as follows:

1. **Profile anonymization**: your first name becomes `[Deleted]`, your last name `User`, and the avatar, phone, and job title fields are cleared
2. **Permanent account ban**: your authentication account is permanently disabled — you can no longer log in
3. **Removal from all organizations**: you are removed from every organization you belonged to
4. **Confirmation email** sent to your address via Brevo

**What is kept after deletion:**

| Data | Treatment |
|------|-----------|
| Authentication record | Kept (anonymized + banned) — required for GDPR traceability |
| Audit logs linked to your account | Kept |
| Tickets you created or were assigned to | Kept — displayed author becomes `[Deleted] User` |
| Messages in tickets | Kept — displayed author becomes `[Deleted] User` |

**What is deleted:**

- Calendar connections (CalDAV, OAuth)
- Active sessions
- Organizational memberships

> ⚠️ **Deletion is irreversible.** No grace period is implemented: once your account is deleted, anonymized data cannot be restored.

### Full deletion (hard delete)

If you require the complete physical deletion of your authentication record (hard delete), contact us via [Contact](/contactus). This operation is not available as self-service and is handled manually by our team.

> If you are the **Owner** of an organization, you must first **transfer ownership** (Settings &gt; Organization) before you can delete your account.

# Account management

The **My account** section lets you manage all your personal information and security preferences. Access it from the user menu at the top right of the interface, then **"My profile"**, or directly via **Settings &gt; My account**.

## Profile information

### Edit your information

From the **Profile** tab, you can edit:

- **First and last name**: displayed in the interface, ticket assignments, and notifications. Enter your full name as you would like it to appear to your colleagues
- **Job title / Role**: your job title (e.g. "IT Manager", "L2 Support Technician"). Displayed on your public profile within the organization
- **Phone number**: used for critical SMS notifications (available on Pro and Enterprise plans)
- **Bio** *(optional)*: a short description visible to other members of your organization

Click **"Save changes"** to confirm your updates.

### Profile picture (Avatar)

To add or change your avatar:

1. Click on your current profile picture (or the default icon)
2. Select an image file from your computer
3. Accepted formats: **JPG, PNG, WebP**
4. Maximum size: **2 MB**
5. The image is automatically cropped to a square format
6. Click **"Save"**

To remove your avatar and revert to the default icon, click **"Remove photo"**.

## Changing your email address

Changing the email address associated with your account requires a two-step verification:

1. Go to **My account &gt; Security &gt; Email**
2. Enter your **new email address**
3. Confirm with your **current password**
4. Click **"Update email"**
5. A confirmation email is sent to the **new** address
6. Click the confirmation link in that email
7. Your email address is updated

> **Important**: Until you confirm the new address, your old email address remains active. If you don't receive the confirmation email, check your spam folder or click **"Resend link"**.

## Changing your password

To change your password:

1. Go to **My account &gt; Security &gt; Password**
2. Enter your **current password**
3. Enter your **new password**, meeting the following criteria:
   - Minimum 8 characters
   - At least one uppercase letter
   - At least one lowercase letter
   - At least one digit
   - Special characters recommended
4. Confirm the new password
5. Click **"Update password"**

A password strength indicator helps you choose a robust password. After changing your password, all your active sessions are kept, but you can revoke them manually.

### Forgotten password

If you've forgotten your password:

1. On the login page, click **"Forgot password?"**
2. Enter the email address for your account
3. An email containing a reset link is sent
4. Click the link (valid for 1 hour) and set a new password

## Account notification preferences

Manage notifications specific to your account:

| Notification type | Description | Email | In-app |
|---------------------|-------------|-------|--------|
| Login from a new device | Alert if your account is used from an unknown device | ✅ | ✅ |
| Password change | Confirmation after a password change | ✅ | — |
| Email change | Confirmation of an address change | ✅ | — |
| Session expiry | Reminder when your session is about to expire | — | ✅ |

To change these preferences, go to **My account &gt; Notifications**.

## Active sessions

The **Active sessions** section displays the list of all open sessions for your account:

- **Device**: type of device and browser used
- **Approximate location**: based on IP address
- **Last activity date**
- **Current session**: marked with a "Current" badge

You can **revoke** an individual session by clicking **"Sign out"**, or revoke all other sessions by clicking **"Sign out of all devices"**.

> **Security tip**: If you spot a session you don't recognize, revoke it immediately and change your password.

## Account deletion

To permanently delete your Hasfy account:

1. Go to **My account &gt; Danger zone**
2. Click **"Delete my account"**
3. A confirmation window warns you of the consequences:
   - Irreversible deletion of all your personal data
   - Removal from all organizations you are a member of
   - If you are the **Owner**, you must transfer ownership before deletion
4. Type **"DELETE"** to confirm
5. Your account is deleted within 30 days (a grace period allowing cancellation)

> **Warning**: Account deletion is irreversible after the 30-day grace period. All associated data will be permanently erased in compliance with GDPR.

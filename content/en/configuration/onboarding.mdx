# Onboarding: Initial setup

When you first log in to Hasfy, an onboarding flow guides you through setting up your workspace. **The flow automatically adapts to your role**: the creator of an organization follows an Owner flow (5 to 6 steps), while an invited member follows a lighter 3-step flow.

---

## Creating an account

Go to **[app.hasfy.fr/auth/signup](https://app.hasfy.fr/auth/signup)** and fill in the registration form:

- **First name** *(required)*
- **Last name** *(required)*
- **Email address** *(required)*
- **Password** *(required)* — visibility can be toggled with the dedicated button

You can also create your account in one click via **Google**, **GitHub**, or **Microsoft** (OAuth).

> **Tip**: If you received an invitation link containing the `?plan=` parameter, the corresponding plan (Starter, Pro, or Max) will be pre-selected automatically at the subscription step.

---

## Owner flow — Organization creator

This flow applies to anyone creating a new Hasfy organization. The creator automatically receives the **Owner** role (non-revocable, but transferable).

### Step 0 — Calibration

The first thing to do is choose your **organization type** between two options shown as visual cards:

- **IT Team**: internal IT team within a company
- **MSP**: managed service provider managing multiple clients

This choice determines the following questions and whether the MSP billing step is shown.

#### IT Team questions (3 questions)

1. **Company size**
   `1-10` | `11-50` | `51-250` | `250+`

2. **Current solution**
   - Nothing / ad hoc
   - Spreadsheets
   - Internal IT team
   - Existing tool *(free text input if selected)*

3. **Main goal** *(with icons)*
   - Monitor device health
   - Strengthen security
   - Simplify employee support
   - Centralize inventory
   - Other *(free text input)*

#### MSP questions (3 questions)

1. **Number of clients managed**
   `1-5` | `6-20` | `21-50` | `50+`

2. **Current tools**
   - None
   - RMM only
   - PSA only
   - RMM + PSA
   - Other *(free text input)*

3. **Main goal**
   - Manage at scale
   - Automate
   - Secure
   - Centralize
   - Other *(free text input)*

> **Note**: Calibration answers are saved to the database. If you return to the flow, you will not be asked them again.

---

### Step 1 — Organization information

Fill in your organization's details:

| Field | Required | Details |
|-------|:--------:|---------|
| Logo | No | Image upload — delete button visible on hover |
| Organization name | **Yes** | Appears in the interface and communications |
| Country | **Yes** | 6 options only: France 🇫🇷, Belgium 🇧🇪, Switzerland 🇨🇭, Luxembourg 🇱🇺, Monaco 🇲🇨, Andorra 🇦🇩 |
| City | **Yes** | Free text input |

The **Continue** button stays disabled until the name, country, and city are all filled in.

> **Important**: The account creator automatically becomes the organization's **Owner**. This role grants all administrative rights. It cannot be revoked but can be transferred to another member.

---

### Step 2 — Team members

Invite members of your team before finalizing the flow. Two methods are available:

#### Manual entry

For each member, fill in:
- Email, First name, Last name, Job title, Role

#### CSV import

Download the provided template and import a file with the following headers:

```
firstName, lastName, email, job, role
```

Validation errors indicate the **line number** where the problem occurred.

#### Assignable roles

| Role | Display label | Limit |
|------|--------------|-------|
| `user` | Employee | — |
| `technician` | Technician | — |
| `admin` | Admin | **2 admins maximum** per organization |

> **Note**: Each organization is limited to **250 users**. You can click **"Do this later"** to skip this step and invite members from the **Users & Groups** page.

---

### Step 3 — Billing (MSP only)

This step is displayed **exclusively** for organizations of type MSP.

One option is available: **Manual billing** (`msp_billing_mode = "manual"`).

---

### Step 3 or 4 — Subscription

Hasfy offers a single plan displayed with a **Beta** badge:

| | Price |
|-|-------|
| Base plan | **€150/user/month** ~~€200~~ |
| AI add-on *(optional)* | +€30/seat/month |

The number of seats is calculated automatically:

```
Seats = max(1, number of team members added + 1)
```
*(the Owner is included in the count)*

Payment is processed via **Mollie** — you are redirected to an external checkout page. Upon returning to Hasfy (`?payment_return=true`), **invitations are automatically sent** to all members added in the previous step.

---

### Final step — Completion

An animated screen confirms your setup is complete, with a 3-item summary:

1. **Invite members** — marked complete if at least one member was added
2. **Configure security** — always marked complete
3. **Billing** — always marked complete

Click **"Go to dashboard"** to be redirected to `/app`.

## Member flow — Invited member

This flow applies to anyone joining an existing organization via an invitation.

### Step 0 — Member calibration

3 questions to personalize your experience:

1. **Your role in the team**
   Admin | Technician | Helpdesk | Field technician

2. **Primary use**
   Monitoring | Tickets | Reporting | All

3. **Familiarity with RMM tools**
   Beginner | Some experience | Expert

---

### Step 1 — Profile

Review or complete your personal information:

- **First name** *(required)* — pre-filled from authentication
- **Last name** *(required)* — pre-filled from authentication

Two preferences to configure:

| Preference | Default |
|------------|:-------:|
| Email notifications | Enabled |
| Dark mode | Disabled |

---

### Step 2 — Notifications

Fine-tune your notification preferences with 4 toggles:

| Notification | Default |
|--------------|:-------:|
| New tickets | Enabled |
| Ticket updates | Enabled |
| Critical alerts | Enabled |
| Weekly report | Disabled |

---

### Final step — Member completion

The final screen displays 3 **online** status indicators (green dots):

- Client monitoring
- Ticket management
- Notifications

Click **"Go to dashboard"** to be redirected to `/app`.

---

## Changing things later

Information entered during onboarding can be updated from the application settings:

| Information | Where to change it |
|-------------|-------------------|
| Personal profile (name) | Settings › My profile |
| Organization logo and name | Settings › Organization |
| Team members | [Users & Groups](/core-features/usermanagement) |
| Notification preferences | Settings › Notifications |
| Dark mode | Settings › Appearance |

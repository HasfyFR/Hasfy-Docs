# Onboarding — Initial setup

When you first log in to Hasfy, an onboarding wizard guides you through setting up your workspace. This multi-step process allows Hasfy to tailor your experience based on the size of your fleet, your role, and your priorities.

## Creating an account

### Sign up with email and password

1. Go to **[app.hasfy.fr/auth/signup](https://app.hasfy.fr/auth/signup)**
2. Enter your **professional email address**
3. Choose a **secure password** that meets the following criteria:
   - Minimum 8 characters
   - At least one uppercase and one lowercase letter
   - At least one digit
   - Special characters are recommended
4. Click **"Create my account"**
5. A confirmation email is sent to the address provided — click the link to verify your account

### Sign up with a magic link

If you'd rather not set a password right away:

1. On the sign-up page, click **"Sign in with magic link"**
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your inbox and click the link you receive
4. You're automatically signed in and redirected to onboarding

> **Note**: You can set a password later from **Settings &gt; My account &gt; Security**.

## Step 1: Personal information

The first step of onboarding asks you to complete your profile:

- **First and last name**: used for display in the interface, ticket assignments, and notifications
- **Phone number** *(optional)*: for critical SMS notifications (if enabled in your plan)
- **Profile picture** *(optional)*: click the default avatar to upload a photo. Accepted formats: JPG, PNG, WebP. Maximum size: 2 MB

## Step 2: Organization setup

This step creates the shared workspace for your team:

- **Organization name**: the name of your company or IT department (e.g. "Acme Corp", "IT Services Lyon"). This name will appear in the application header and in communications
- **Industry**: select your industry from the proposed list (Healthcare, Finance, Education, Industry, Services, Retail, Technology, Other). This information helps Hasfy tailor certain recommendations
- **Company size**: indicate the relevant range (1-10, 11-50, 51-200, 201-500, 500+)
- **Country**: your organization's primary location

> **Important**: The account creator automatically becomes the **Owner** of the organization. This role grants all administrative rights, including billing management and organization deletion. This role cannot be revoked but can be transferred.

## Step 3: Calibration questions

Hasfy asks a series of questions to tailor the interface to your context:

### IT fleet size
- How many devices do you manage? (1-25, 26-100, 101-500, 500+)
- This information helps adjust default limits and display recommendations

### Top priorities
Select your priorities from:
- **Inventory and fleet management**: asset tracking, lifecycle, assignments
- **Monitoring and supervision**: real-time alerts, performance metrics
- **Ticketing and support**: incident management, intervention tracking
- **Reporting and KPIs**: dashboards, performance indicators
- **All of the above**: full configuration

### Your role
- IT Manager / CIO
- Technician / Support
- MSP provider
- Manager / Management
- Other

### Experience with ITSM tools
- First time using a tool of this kind
- Occasional use of similar tools
- Experienced user

> **Note**: These answers have no impact on the available features. They are used solely to personalize the initial experience (contextual tutorials, order of featured modules, complexity of default views).

## Step 4: Inviting your team

Before completing onboarding, you can invite the first members of your team:

1. Enter the **email addresses** of the people you want to invite (one per line or separated by commas)
2. For each invitee, select a **role**:
   - **Admin**: full access to organization management (except billing and deletion)
   - **Technician**: management of equipment, tickets, and interventions
   - **Employee**: read-only access, with the ability to create tickets
3. Click **"Send invitations"**

Invitees receive an email containing a link to join your organization. They will need to create their own Hasfy account if they don't already have one.

> **Tip**: You can skip this step and invite members later from **Settings &gt; Users &gt; Invite**.

## Step 5: Finishing up

Once all the steps are complete:

1. A summary of your configuration is displayed
2. Click **"Start using Hasfy"**
3. You are redirected to your organization's **dashboard**

Onboarding then displays **contextual tutorials** (tooltips) during your first navigation to guide you through discovering the interface. You can disable these tutorials at any time from **Settings &gt; Preferences &gt; Tutorials**.

## Changing things later

All the information provided during onboarding can be changed afterwards:

| Information | Where to change it |
|-------------|----------------|
| Personal profile | Settings &gt; My profile |
| Organization name | Settings &gt; Organization |
| Industry and size | Settings &gt; Organization |
| Team members | Settings &gt; Users |
| Display preferences | Settings &gt; Preferences |

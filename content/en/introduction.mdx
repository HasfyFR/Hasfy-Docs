# Introduction to Hasfy

## What is Hasfy?

Hasfy is a **French SaaS platform for IT fleet management and monitoring** (IT Asset Management & Monitoring). It enables IT teams, managed service providers (MSPs), and businesses to centralize the management of all their IT assets within a modern, intuitive, and secure interface.

Accessible from **app.hasfy.fr**, Hasfy combines in a single tool the essential functions that previously required several separate pieces of software: fleet inventory, real-time monitoring, ticketing, intervention scheduling, and analytical dashboards.

## Why Hasfy?

### The problem

Managing an IT fleet traditionally involves a multitude of fragmented tools: a spreadsheet for inventory, a separate ticketing tool, a distinct monitoring solution, email exchanges for interventions… This fragmentation leads to:

- **Loss of visibility**: information is scattered and hard to cross-reference
- **Wasted time**: duplicate data entry and switching between tools slow teams down
- **Limited responsiveness**: without centralized monitoring, problems are detected late
- **Difficult collaboration**: technicians, administrators, and customers use different channels

### The Hasfy solution

Hasfy unifies all of these functions in a single platform:

| Need | Hasfy Solution |
|--------|---------------|
| Fleet inventory | Equipment module with tags, lifecycle, and assignment |
| Real-time monitoring | Hasfy Agent (built on Elastic Agent) installed on every machine |
| Ticketing | Ticket system with priorities, statuses, conversations, and SLAs |
| Scheduling | Built-in calendar with maintenance windows and recurrence |
| Reporting | KPI dashboard with filters and customizable date ranges |
| Collaboration | Roles and permissions, internal notes, real-time notifications |

## Core features

### Equipment management
Keep a complete, up-to-date inventory of your entire IT fleet: workstations, servers, printers, network equipment, mobile devices. Each piece of equipment has a detailed record including its hardware specifications, intervention history, health metrics, and lifecycle status.

### Agent-based monitoring
The **Hasfy Agent** — a lightweight agent built on Elastic Agent — installs on your Windows, macOS, and Linux machines via a desktop installer built with Tauri. Once deployed, the agent continuously reports system metrics (CPU, RAM, disk, network, uptime) to Hasfy, enabling proactive monitoring of your fleet.

### Ticketing system
Create support tickets directly from the Hasfy interface or receive them from your users. Each ticket can be assigned to a technician, prioritized, categorized, and tracked through a complete resolution workflow. Built-in conversations and internal notes make collaboration easier.

### Dashboard & KPIs
Get a quick overview of your fleet's overall health with key performance indicators: number of active equipment, ticket resolution rates, ongoing alerts, system performance metrics. Filters and date ranges let you analyze trends.

### Calendar
Plan your maintenance interventions, schedule recurring maintenance windows, and coordinate your team with the built-in calendar. Each event can be linked to a specific ticket or piece of equipment.

### Smart notifications
Receive real-time alerts in the app and by email whenever an important event occurs: monitoring alert, new ticket, status change, approaching SLA deadline. Configure your preferences to receive only the notifications that matter to you.

### Artificial intelligence
Hasfy's AI assistant analyzes your tickets to suggest solutions, automatically categorizes incoming requests, and identifies failure trends for predictive maintenance.

## Who is Hasfy for?

### Corporate IT managers
You manage the IT fleet of an SMB or a large group. Hasfy gives you **complete visibility** into the state of your equipment, automates intervention tracking, and provides the KPIs needed for reporting.

### Managed service providers (MSPs)
You manage the fleets of multiple clients. Hasfy lets you **centralize multi-client management** with strict data separation per organization, while giving your clients a portal to track interventions.

### Support teams and technicians
You handle incidents on a daily basis. Hasfy organizes your workload with **smart ticketing**, an intervention calendar, and diagnostic information reported by the agent.

### IT departments (CIOs)
You drive IT strategy. Hasfy provides the **decision-making indicators** needed to optimize budgets, anticipate renewals, and measure support performance.

## Platform architecture

Hasfy is built on a modern, high-performance architecture:

- **Web application** accessible from any modern browser (app.hasfy.fr)
- **Lightweight desktop agent** for monitoring workstations (Windows, macOS, Linux)
- **REST API** for integration with your existing tools
- **Secure infrastructure** hosted in France with data encryption

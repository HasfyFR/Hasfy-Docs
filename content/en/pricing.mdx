## Billing

### Billing modes

- **Monthly**: charged each month on the subscription date
- **Annual**: a single charge with **2 months free** (equivalent to 10 months for 12 months of access)

### Per-seat billing

The Starter plan is billed **per active user**. Every user added to your organization (regardless of role) counts as a seat. Additions and removals during the month are calculated **pro rata**.

**Example**: 8 users on the monthly Starter plan = 8 × €5 = €40/month

### Payment methods

Accepted credit cards: **Visa**, **Mastercard**, **American Express**. Payments are secured by Stripe with 3D Secure authentication.

For Enterprise plans: payment by **bank transfer** and custom invoicing are available.

## Free trial

All plans come with a **14-day free trial**:

- No credit card required
- Access to all Starter plan features
- Data retained for 30 days after the trial ends
- Seamless transition to a paid plan (no data loss)

## Frequently asked pricing questions

### What happens if I exceed the user limit?

You'll receive a notification when you approach the limit. To add more users, you can upgrade to a higher plan. The plan change takes effect immediately.

### Can I increase only the equipment limit without changing plans?

Currently, limits are tied to plans. If you need more equipment but no other features, contact us for a custom quote.

### Are there any hidden fees?

No. The listed price includes all the features shown. There are no setup, training, or cancellation fees. VAT is applied in addition, in accordance with applicable law (20% for mainland France).

### Do you offer pricing for non-profits or educational institutions?

Yes. Hasfy offers **preferential pricing** for non-profit organizations and educational institutions. Contact us at **sales@hasfy.fr** to take advantage of it.

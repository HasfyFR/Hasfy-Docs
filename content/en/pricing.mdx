# Pricing

Hasfy offers **a single plan, Hasfy Pro**, billed **per seat** (billable user). No tiers, no features locked behind a higher plan: you pay for your administrator and technician accounts, and everything else — monitoring, equipment, sites, custom roles — is included.

Hasfy is currently in **Early Access**: early accounts get a guaranteed reduced rate for as long as their account stays active.

## How it works

Hasfy's billing model is built on two simple building blocks:

1. **The seat** — a billable account, i.e. a user with an **administrator** or **technician** role ("superuser"). Every seat you purchase automatically unlocks **250 free standard user accounts** (employees, read/everyday access, non-billable) to attach to your organization.
2. **The AI Assistant add-on (optional)** — an opt-in option per user, on top of your base subscription, that adds AI-driven summaries, suggestions, and equipment analysis.

In short: you only pay for the accounts that actually drive your IT operations (admins and technicians), not for every person who looks at a ticket or a piece of equipment in your organization.

## AI Assistant add-on (optional)

The **AI Assistant** is an option you can enable on top of your base subscription:

| | |
|---|---|
| **Price** | **+€30/month per user**, on top of the Hasfy Pro subscription |
| **Billing mode** | Flat-rate per user — you don't pay per token, regardless of actual usage |
| **Quota included in the Pro plan** | **3,000,000 AI tokens per month** |

What the AI Assistant brings to the table:

- **Automatic summaries** — quick recaps of tickets, incidents, and equipment history
- **Suggestions** — action and prioritization recommendations for your team
- **Equipment analysis** — intelligent reading of monitoring data to spot trends and anomalies

> The 3,000,000 tokens/month quota included in the Pro plan comfortably covers everyday use. If you go over it, **the AI Assistant remains fully usable**: the token counter shown in your workspace is purely **indicative**, just to track your consumption — it neither blocks usage nor bills you for going over.

## 14-day free trial

Before committing, try Hasfy Pro under real conditions:

- **14 days free**, **€0 at signup**
- A **credit card is required** to start the trial (direct debit mandate), but **nothing is charged before the trial ends**
- **Cancellable at any time during the trial**, at no cost: if you cancel before the 14 days are up, your card won't be charged
- At the end of the trial, your subscription **automatically switches to the standard rate of your chosen plan** (with or without the AI Assistant add-on, depending on what you enabled during the trial)

> Remember to enable or disable the AI Assistant *before* the end of your trial if you want to adjust your setup before the switch to a paid subscription.

## Frequently asked pricing questions

### How does payment for my Hasfy subscription work?

Hasfy subscriptions are billed and collected via **Mollie**, which supports credit cards as well as other payment methods depending on your country. The charge is collected automatically on each billing date.

### What happens if I add or remove seats mid-month?

- **Adding a seat**: the cost is calculated **pro rata** for the remaining time in your current billing cycle, and charged immediately.
- **Removing a seat**: the removal **takes effect at the next renewal** — you keep using the seat until the end of the period you've already paid for, with no partial refund mid-cycle.

### What happens at the end of my 14-day free trial?

If you haven't cancelled, your subscription automatically starts at the **standard rate of your chosen plan** — currently the Early Access rate of €150/month per seat (plus €30/month/user if you enabled the AI Assistant). No action is required on your part: the trial converts directly into an active subscription.

### Can I bill my own clients through Hasfy?

Today, billing your end clients (on the portal side) works in **manual mode by default**: you generate and send your invoices as PDFs, payment is made by **bank transfer**, and you set your own commercial terms with your clients. Online card payment for your client invoices **is not yet automated** in Hasfy — this feature is on our roadmap.

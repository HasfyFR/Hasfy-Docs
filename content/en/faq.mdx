# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Here you'll find answers to the most frequently asked questions about Hasfy. If you can't find what you're looking for, [contact our support team](/contactus).

## Account & Sign-up

### How do I create a Hasfy account?

Go to **[app.hasfy.fr](https://app.hasfy.fr)** and click "Create an account". You can sign up with an email and password, or use a magic link sent by email. A 14-day free trial is included (a credit card is required for the direct debit mandate, but nothing is charged before the trial ends). See the [quick start guide](/quickstart) for detailed steps.

### Can I use Hasfy without a credit card?

A credit card is required to start your 14-day free trial (direct debit mandate), but **nothing is charged before the trial ends**. You can explore all the features of the Hasfy Pro plan during this period, and cancel at any time at no cost. See the [Pricing](/pricing) page for the full details.

### How do I reset my password?

On the login page, click "Forgot password?", enter your email address, and follow the reset link you receive by email. The link is valid for 1 hour. See the [Account Management](/configuration/account) page for more details.

### Can I belong to multiple organizations?

Currently, a Hasfy account is associated with **a single organization**. If you manage multiple organizations, you'll need to create separate accounts with different email addresses. Multi-organization support is planned for a future release.

## Features

### What is the Hasfy agent, and is it required?

The Hasfy agent is lightweight software installed on the machines in your fleet to report monitoring metrics (CPU, RAM, disk, network) in real time. It is **not required**: you can use Hasfy purely as an inventory and ticketing tool without installing the agent. The agent adds the monitoring dimension. See the [installation guide](/quickstart#step-4-install-the-hasfy-agent).

### Which operating systems does the agent support?

The Hasfy agent is compatible with:
- **Windows** 10 and later
- **macOS** 12 (Monterey) and later
- **Linux**: Debian/Ubuntu (.deb) and RHEL/Fedora (.rpm) distributions

### How many pieces of equipment can I manage?

The number of monitored devices is **unlimited** on the Hasfy Pro plan: the cost of monitoring is already built into the seat price, with no separate counting or tier to exceed. See the [Pricing](/pricing) page for the full details.

### How does the ticketing system work?

The ticketing system lets you create, assign, and track incidents and requests. Each ticket has a status (New, In Progress, Pending, Resolved, Closed), a priority, and can be assigned to a technician. Conversations, internal notes, and attachments are built in. See the [Support & Tickets](/core-features/assistance) guide.

### Is the AI Assistant included in my subscription?

The AI Assistant is an **optional add-on**: +€30/month per billable seat (Administrator or Technician account), on top of your Hasfy Pro subscription, billed at a flat rate (not per token). Powered by Mistral AI, it generates on-demand **ticket summaries**, suggests **solutions** drawn from your knowledge base, and produces **diagnostic reports** on your monitored equipment. The Pro plan includes a quota of 3,000,000 AI tokens per month. Beyond that, the AI Assistant remains usable and the displayed tracking stays purely indicative. See [AI Features](/advanced-features/ia) and the [Pricing](/pricing) page.

### Can I export my data?

Yes. Several exports are available directly from the interface, per module: the equipment fleet (**Equipment** page), tickets (**Support** page), and the audit log (**Settings &gt; Audit**), in CSV, JSON, or Markdown formats. The REST API also lets you export your data programmatically. See [Data & Backups](/settings-security/databackup).

## Billing & Subscription

### What payment methods are accepted?

Hasfy subscriptions are billed and collected via **Mollie**, which supports credit cards as well as other payment methods depending on your country. The charge is collected automatically on each due date.

### Can I add or remove seats at any time?

Yes. Hasfy offers only one plan, **Hasfy Pro**, billed per seat (admin/technician account). **Adding** a seat takes effect immediately and is billed pro rata for the remaining time in your current cycle. **Removing** a seat takes effect at the next renewal: you keep using it until the end of the period you've already paid for. See [Billing & Payment](/configuration/billing) and the [Pricing](/pricing) page.

### How does per-seat billing work?

Hasfy charges **€150/month per seat** at the current Early Access rate (€200/month at the standard rate, guaranteed for as long as your account stays active). Each seat automatically unlocks 250 free standard user accounts. Adding a seat mid-month is calculated pro rata; removing one takes effect at the next renewal. See the [Pricing](/pricing) page for the full details.

### Where can I find my invoices?

All your invoices are available under **Settings &gt; Billing &gt; Invoice History**. You can view them online and download them as PDFs.

### What happens if I cancel my subscription?

Access to features remains available until the end of the current billing period. Make sure to export any important data before the end of that period. For any questions about data retention after cancellation, contact us via [Contact](/contactus).

## Security

### Is my data secure?

Yes. Hasfy applies the following measures, among others:
- All communications (web app, agent, API) are encrypted via **HTTPS/TLS**
- Two-factor authentication (2FA) is available for all accounts
- Hosting with European providers (Supabase, OVHcloud)

See [Data & Backups](/settings-security/databackup) and the [security best practices](/settings-security/goodpratices) for more details.

### Is Hasfy GDPR compliant?

Yes. Hasfy respects the rights provided for under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR): access, rectification, erasure, portability, and objection. To exercise any of these rights, contact us via the [Contact](/contactus) page. See [Data & Backups](/settings-security/databackup).

### How do I enable two-factor authentication?

Open the **Settings** window, **Security** section, click "Enable 2FA" and scan the displayed QR code with an authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, etc.), then confirm with the generated 6-digit code. See the [Two-factor authentication](/settings-security/mfas) guide.

## Technical questions

### Does the agent consume a lot of resources?

No. The Hasfy agent is designed to be lightweight:
- **CPU**: &lt; 1% average usage
- **RAM**: about 50-80 MB
- **Network**: a few KB per minute (sending metrics every 60 seconds)
- **Disk**: about 100 MB of installation space

### Does the agent work behind a proxy or firewall?

The agent communicates over HTTPS (port 443) with Hasfy's servers. If your network uses a proxy, you can configure the agent to use it during enrollment. Make sure the `*.hasfy.fr` domain is allowed through your firewall.

### Can I use the API to automate tasks?

Yes. The Hasfy REST API lets you read and write data programmatically: create equipment, open tickets, retrieve metrics, and more. See the [API documentation](/api) for endpoints and examples.

### How long is monitoring data retained?

Monitoring metrics reported by the agent are retained for as long as your subscription is active. For any questions about data retention, contact us via [Contact](/contactus).

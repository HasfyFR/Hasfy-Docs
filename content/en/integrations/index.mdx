# Integrations

Hasfy connects to your IT ecosystem to centralize fleet management and automate your workflows. This page covers what's natively included in your Hasfy Pro subscription, the integrations available today in the marketplace, and what's on our roadmap.

## Included in Hasfy Pro

Two essential capabilities are part of the Hasfy Pro foundation and are **not integrations to activate**. They're available as soon as your organization is created, with no extra setup.

### Monitoring agent

Installed on every piece of equipment you want to monitor, the Hasfy agent automatically collects system metrics (CPU, RAM, disk, network, uptime) and reports them to your dashboard.

➡️ [Install the Hasfy agent](/quickstart#step-4-install-the-hasfy-agent)

### REST API

The Hasfy REST API is included with the Pro subscription and accessible via API keys, from **Settings &gt; API**.

➡️ [Hasfy API documentation](/api)

## Integrations marketplace

All integrations are managed from the **Integrations** page, accessible from the main navigation menu (**Automation** section, puzzle icon 🧩). This page is restricted to the **Owner** and **Admin** roles.

It's a marketplace organized into categories, accessible via tabs:

All · Accounting · Communication · Security · Monitoring · CRM · Productivity · Payment · Calendar

![Integrations page: marketplace view with category and status grid](/images/integration-page.jpg)

A search bar lets you filter integrations by name. Each integration displays one of the following statuses:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|----------------|
| **Connected** | The integration is active on your organization |
| **Available** | Can be activated immediately ("Activate" / "Manage" button) |
| **In development** | Announced and under development, not yet usable |
| **Coming soon** | On the roadmap, no availability date communicated |

## Available today

### Mollie Connect: Payment

**Mollie Connect** is, as of today, the only integration that can be activated from the marketplace. It lets you connect your own Mollie account to charge your clients:

- Create **payment links** for your client invoices
- **Real-time tracking** of payment status
- **Issue invoices** directly from Hasfy
- Manage your Mollie **payment profiles**

> **Don't confuse this with**: Mollie Connect is about charging **your clients**, via your own Mollie account. Payment for **your Hasfy Pro subscription** also uses Mollie, but is managed separately from [Settings &gt; Billing](/configuration/billing).

➡️ [Set up Mollie Connect](/integrations/mollie-connect)

# Stripe Integration

Hasfy uses **Stripe** as its payment provider to securely manage subscriptions, billing, and payment methods. This page details the billing features available from Hasfy.

## Overview

The Stripe integration is **native** to Hasfy — it is automatically active for all organizations with a paid subscription. No additional configuration is required.

### Features covered

| Feature | Description |
|---------------|-------------|
| Subscription | Choose and subscribe to a plan (Starter, Pro, Enterprise) |
| Recurring billing | Automatic monthly or annual charges |
| Payment methods | Credit card, SEPA (depending on availability) |
| Invoices | Generation and download of invoices |
| Customer portal | Self-service management of payment information |
| Seat management | Adding/removing billed users |
| Plan changes | Self-service upgrade or downgrade |

## Subscribing to a plan

### Choosing a plan

1. Go to **Settings &gt; Billing &gt; Subscription**
2. Review the available plans:

| Plan | Price | Users | Equipment | Metrics |
|------|------|-------------|-------------|-----------|
| **Starter** | €5/user/month | Up to 20 | 100 | 90 days |
| **Pro** | On request | Up to 50 | 500 | 1 year |
| **Enterprise** | Custom quote | Unlimited | Unlimited | 2 years |

3. Click **"Choose this plan"**
4. You are redirected to the secure Stripe payment form

### Billing cycle

When subscribing, choose your billing cycle:

- **Monthly**: charged each month on the subscription date
- **Annual**: charged annually with **2 months free** (i.e. 10 months billed for 12 months of access)

> **Tip**: The annual subscription offers significant savings. If you're satisfied after your first month, switch to annual from **Settings &gt; Billing &gt; Billing cycle**.

### Free trial

Hasfy offers a **14-day free trial** on the Starter plan — no credit card required. At the end of the trial period, you can:

- Subscribe to a paid plan to continue
- Let the account expire (your data is retained for 30 days)

## Payment methods

### Credit card

The primary payment method is a credit card:

- **Visa**, **Mastercard**, and **American Express** accepted
- Payments are secured via Stripe with **3D Secure authentication** when required by your bank
- Your card number is never stored by Hasfy — it is managed exclusively by Stripe

### Adding or changing a payment method

1. Go to **Settings &gt; Billing &gt; Payment methods**
2. Click **"Add a card"** or **"Edit"**
3. Enter your card information in the secure Stripe form
4. The new payment method replaces the old one for future charges

### Payment failure

In the event of a payment failure (expired card, insufficient funds, bank rejection):

1. **Notification**: you receive an email alerting you to the failure
2. **Automatic retry**: Stripe automatically retries the payment after 3, 5, and 7 days
3. **Update**: update your payment method to avoid interruption
4. **Suspension**: after 3 consecutive failures (about 14 days), access to paid features is suspended
5. **Reactivation**: update your card and the payment is retried immediately

> **Important**: Your data is never deleted in the event of a suspension. Only access to features is restricted. Once payment is settled, full access is restored instantly.

## Invoices

### Viewing invoices

All your invoices are accessible from **Settings &gt; Billing &gt; Invoice history**:

| Information | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| Invoice number | Unique invoice identifier |
| Date | Issue date |
| Amount | Total amount including tax |
| Status | Paid, Pending, Unpaid |
| Actions | Download (PDF), View in Stripe |

### Downloading

Click **"Download"** next to an invoice to get the PDF. The invoice includes:

- Hasfy's contact details and your organization's
- Plan details and number of seats
- The amount excluding tax, VAT, and the total amount including tax
- The payment method used

### Billing information

To change the information shown on your invoices (company name, address, VAT number):

1. Go to **Settings &gt; Billing &gt; Billing information**
2. Edit the desired fields:
   - Legal company name
   - Billing address
   - Intra-community VAT number
   - Email address for receiving invoices
3. Click **"Save"**

Changes apply to future invoices. Invoices already issued are not modified.

## Changing plans

### Upgrading

To move to a higher plan:

1. Go to **Settings &gt; Billing &gt; Subscription**
2. Click **"Change plan"**
3. Select the desired plan
4. Confirm the change

The upgrade is **immediate**: you instantly benefit from the new features. The amount is calculated on a **prorated** basis for the remaining period.

### Downgrading

To move to a lower plan:

1. Same procedure as for an upgrade
2. The downgrade takes effect at the **end of the current billing period**
3. Make sure you comply with the limits of the new plan (number of users, equipment)

> **Warning**: If your usage exceeds the limits of the lower plan (e.g. 30 users for a plan limited to 20), you must reduce usage before the downgrade takes effect. Hasfy will notify you of the necessary adjustments.

## Seat management

### Per-seat billing

On the Starter plan, the number of users directly affects the cost:

- **Base price**: €5 per user per month
- **Adding a user**: when you invite a new user, the cost is automatically adjusted on a prorated basis
- **Removing a user**: when a user is removed, a credit is applied to the next invoice

### Viewing current usage

From **Settings &gt; Billing &gt; Usage**, you can view:

- Current number of active users
- Plan limit
- Estimated cost for the next invoice
- History of seat count changes

## Stripe customer portal

For advanced management, access the **Stripe customer portal** directly:

1. Go to **Settings &gt; Billing**
2. Click **"Manage on Stripe"**
3. The Stripe portal opens in a new tab

From the Stripe portal, you can:
- Update your payment methods
- View and download all your invoices
- Manage your subscription
- Cancel your subscription

## Cancelling your subscription

To cancel your Hasfy subscription:

1. Go to **Settings &gt; Billing &gt; Subscription**
2. Click **"Cancel subscription"**
3. Indicate the reason for cancellation *(optional but appreciated)*
4. Confirm the cancellation

The cancellation takes effect at the **end of the current billing period**. You retain access to all features until that date. After expiration, see the [data retention policy](/settings-security/databackup) for retention periods.
